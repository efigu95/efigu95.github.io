Hi

I'm Elias, a recent graduate from Florida International University with a Bachelor's in Computer Engineering and I decided to play around with web developemnt (as you probably already noticed).

My interests include (but are not limited to) anything involving good ol' tech. I also enjoy video games, Netflix and being an amateur musician. Most of my time these days is consumed by trying to get a job and well ... making this website.

About 8 years ago I enrolled into Coral Park Senior Highschool, well-known for their Engineering magnet program and their heavy involvment with FIU's College of Engineering and Computing. About 3 years after that I was introduced to the wonders of programming in a dual-enrollment class with FIU; The class was called C For Engineers and is what made me decide to pursue an education in Software Development.

My starting language was C and I fell in love with it. To this day C is my favorite langauage, with Swift coming in at a close 2nd. My 4 years at FIU have taught me to become adaptive - being forced to learn new langauges, technologies and techniques on the fly when called for. They have also taught me that teamwork can accomplish a lot, and can accomplish even more if everyone is on the same page ...
